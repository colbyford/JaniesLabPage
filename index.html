Supramap

Geospatial Phylogenetic Software

Supramap is a tool to generate and share knowledge about evolution and biogeography. A supramap gives users a quick and easy way to integrate genotypic and phenotypic data in a geospatial context. When viewed in a virtual globe (e.g. Google Earth or NASA WorldWind), the user has an interactive map of the spread of various lineages of organisms (e.g. strains of pathogens) over the earth.

EchinoTOL

Assembling the Echinoderm Tree of Life

The tree of life is incomplete without inclusion of the diverse marine animal phylum Echinodermata. To better understand the phylogeny of Echinodermata, we have built a team to consider the fossil and the living echinoderms together.

About the Janies Lab.

The work of the Janies lab involves empirical studies of organismal diversity. Funded by various government organizations such as DARPA, DTRA, and DOD, the lab develops and manages software to track pathogens and visualize diverse genomic data efficiently.
